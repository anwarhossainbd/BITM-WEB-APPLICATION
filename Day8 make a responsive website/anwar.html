Kishoreganj District

Kishoreganj (Bengali: কিশোরগঞ্জ) is a district in Dhaka Division of Bangladesh. Earlier it was a Mohkuma (মহকুমা) under the Mymensingh district. Kishorganj was made a separate district due to Mymensingh dominion suppression. So to suppress Mymensingh, Greater Mymensingh was separate from their 7 out of 6 upazila Kishorgonj, Tangail, Sherpur, Jamalpur, Netrokona( Presently known as District). from Mymensingh. The district consists of four municipalities, 13 upazilas, 105 union parishads, 39 wards, 145 mahallas, 946 mouzas and 1775 villages.
